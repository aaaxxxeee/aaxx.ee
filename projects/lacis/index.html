lacis

“Place des Arts’ screen mosaic, a unique installation composed of 35 digital screens and light tubes, presents six digital artworks on the theme of Labyrinthe, created by the UQAM’s School of Visual and Media Arts.”⠀Village numérique

Lacis is a contemplative piece meant to mimick the flow of foot traffic in the area. The digital ribbons flow indefinitely in a seamless loop, they come and go and disappear in the darkness only to come back a few seconds later.

Originally run from may 1st to june 21, the project was re-run for the 26th edition of the MUTEK festival as part of the “Village numérique”
