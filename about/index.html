about me

Hi I’m Alexandre Beuzen, a Master of Design student at Concordia University where I currently focus on experimental game design.

I’m interested in everything related to media arts. I have a self-taught background in graphic design, but I quickly turned my focus to immersive and interactive installations during my time at the University of Quebec in Montréal.

If you enjoy my work, please don’t hesitate to reach out to me.
